## Five Key Insights

### 1. Gaming Studios Are Scaling Fastest

Klarna, Kindred, Paradox, Gears of Leo have highest permit-to-employee ratios. Urgent housing needs.

### 2. Software Developers Dominate

4,986 permits (23%) are for developers. IT roles combined = 35%+.

### 3. Kista Location Is Perfect

Ericsson (1,290) + Huawei (80) are in Kista, next to your property.

### 4. Scania Is Reachable

1,162 permits in Södertälje, only 35km from Sollentuna.

### 5. 143 Small Companies Are Untapped

Companies with 5-15 permits: big enough for needs, too small for formal programs.
