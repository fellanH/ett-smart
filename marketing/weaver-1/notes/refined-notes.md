### **Project Scope: Corporate Housing Lead Generation**

**Client:** Dag Hellström – *Ett Smart Hotell*
**Goal:** Identify high-potential target audiences for long-term corporate apartment rentals (3–12+ months) in Stockholm.

#### **1. The Strategy**

* **The "Trigger Event":** Companies that have recently applied for and received approved work permits for employees (sourced from *Migrationsverket*) have a verified, immediate need to house staff coming from abroad.
* **The Opportunity:** These companies likely require temporary, long-term accommodation (3–12 months) for project workers or relocating employees.
* **The Solution:** *Ett Smart Hotell* provides exactly this type of housing.

#### **2. Source Material**

* **Input:** Raw CSV export from the *Migrationsverket* (Swedish Migration Agency) database.
* **Contents:** List of companies in Sweden with approved work permits for employees.

#### **3. Action Plan: Data Enrichment**

We need to take the raw company list and enrich it with the following data points to create a usable prospect list:

* **Financial Health:** Company Annual Turnover (to ensure they can afford corporate rates).
* **Geography:** Company Location/HQ (prioritizing those with operations in Stockholm or project sites there).
* **Key Decision Makers:** Identify specific roles responsible for booking housing:
* HR Managers / Mobility Specialists
* Project Managers
* C-Level Management (for smaller firms)



#### **4. Target Persona**

We are looking for companies that match the following profile:

* **Need:** Regular booking of temporary apartments for work-related reasons.
* **Duration:** Long-term stays (3–12+ months).
* **Scenario:** Companies managing projects in Stockholm but with teams based elsewhere (requiring travel/relocation).

#### **5. Deliverable & Purpose**

**Primary Output:** A segmented list of target companies and clear "Target Audience Definitions."
**Why:** To prepare the client (Dag) for a strategic meeting with their marketing agency. The client needs to walk into that meeting with clear directions on **who** the agency should be targeting to maximize their ad spend and outreach efforts.
