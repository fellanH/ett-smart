# LinkedIn Contact Research Guide

## For: Ett Smart Hotell Prospect List

This guide provides instructions for researching and adding key contacts to the prospect list.

---

## Target Contact Roles (Priority Order)

1. **Global Mobility Manager / Relocation Specialist**
   - Search terms: "global mobility", "relocation", "expatriate", "international assignment"

2. **HR Manager / People Operations**
   - Search terms: "HR manager", "people operations", "human resources director", "talent acquisition"

3. **Office Manager / Executive Assistant**
   - Search terms: "office manager", "facilities", "workplace", "executive assistant to CEO"

4. **General Management** (for smaller firms < 100 employees)
   - Search terms: "CEO", "managing director", "country manager Sweden"

---

## LinkedIn Search Process

### Step 1: Company Search

1. Go to LinkedIn
2. Search for company name
3. Click on company page
4. Navigate to "People" tab

### Step 2: Role Search

1. In the search box on the People page, enter target role keywords
2. Filter by "Stockholm" or company HQ location
3. Look for senior-level contacts (Manager, Director, Head of)

### Step 3: Record Information

For each contact found, record:

- **Full Name**
- **Title** (exact as shown on LinkedIn)
- **LinkedIn URL** (format: linkedin.com/in/username)

## Tips for Success

1. **Start with company pages** - Often easier than name searches
2. **Check "Similar profiles"** - LinkedIn suggests related roles
3. **Use Boolean search** - "global mobility" OR "relocation" OR "expatriate"
4. **Note alternatives** - If no mobility manager, get HR manager
5. **Check connections** - Some profiles visible only to 2nd+ connections
6. **Export as you go** - Don't lose progress
